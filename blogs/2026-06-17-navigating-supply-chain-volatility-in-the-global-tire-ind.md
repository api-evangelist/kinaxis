The tire industry faces mounting supply chain challenges including raw material volatility, logistics bottlenecks, and sustainability pressures. Kinaxis argues these can be addressed by adopting adaptive supply chains that use concurrent decision-making and autonomous orchestration to respond in real time rather than relying on static sequential planning.
